4. The absence of sharp spikes or high-impact time windows indicates that the issue is not driven by a temporary outage or time-based anomaly.

5. Instead, the pattern suggests a consistent, systemic behavior affecting Stripe transactions.

6. Possible causes include:

   - Configuration or routing issues affecting Stripe transactions
